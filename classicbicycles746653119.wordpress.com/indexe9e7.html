For the Love of Bikes

You can’t buy happiness but you can buy a bike and that’s pretty close.

In our family friendly bike shop, join the vintage or road writers bicycle clubs and then shop for pro gear and bicycles and learn from the pros.

Our Shop

Our family oriented shop includes a retail store where you will be greeted by our friendly stuff and made to feel right at home. You can grab a smoothie at our smoothie bar and browse all the gear you are looking to use during our next ride on the road or at the beach. You will also find a repair shop where our clients bring their bikes for tune ups and repairs or adjustments.While you wait, sign up for a class or one of our popular bicycle clubs.

Our Clubs

Our clubs something for everyone. Our Road Riders Club offers an opportunity to participate in scheduled training rides with other athletes, and our vintage Cycle Club offers an opportunity for leisure writers to join together and enjoy a scheduled scenic ride. Visit the club’s page to learn more and check out the riding schedule and sign up to join a ride.
